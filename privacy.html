Privacy Policy

Mopy Chrome Extension

Data Collection

Mopy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. All text snippets and folders are stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your computer.
  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • Data may sync with your Chrome profile across devices (standard Chrome functionality).
  • You can delete all data by removing the extension.
